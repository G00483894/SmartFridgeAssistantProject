# SmartFridgeAssistantProject

## About the Project

This project is a 3-page website created for a fictional product called **Smart Fridge Assistant**.

The idea behind the product is simple — it helps people manage food in their fridge. It can track expiry dates, suggest recipes, and create shopping lists automatically.

The website is made to explain the product in a clear and simple way and show why it would be useful in everyday life.

---

## Who It’s For

The product is designed mainly for:
* students
* families
* busy people

Basically, anyone who wants to save time and avoid wasting food.

---

## The Problem

A lot of people forget what they have in their fridge. Food goes bad, money gets wasted, and shopping becomes messy.

---

## The Solution

Smart Fridge Assistant helps by:

* reminding you before food expires
* creating shopping lists automatically
* suggesting meals based on what you already have

---

## Website Pages

The website has 3 pages:

### Home (`index.html`)

* introduction to the product
* main benefits
* simple call-to-action

### Features (`features.html`)

* explanation of how the system works
* key features of the product

### Order (`order.html`)

* product selection
* form for delivery details
* order confirmation
