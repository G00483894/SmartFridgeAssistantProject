# Project Brief – Smart Fridge Assistant Website

## Project Title
**Smart Fridge Assistant**

## Overview

For this project, I will design a **3-page website** for a fictional IoT product called **Smart Fridge Assistant**.

Smart Fridge Assistant is a smart refrigerator system for homes and apartments. It helps people manage their food better by tracking expiry dates, creating shopping lists, and suggesting recipes based on the food already in the fridge.

The website will promote the product and explain how it works in a simple and clear way.

## Target Audience
This product is designed for:

* students
* families
* busy people

These users often want to save time, reduce food waste, and make shopping easier.

## Problem It Solves
A common problem in many homes is that food expires before people use it. This causes food waste and also wastes money.

People may also forget what food they already have and buy things they do not need.

## Product Benefits
Smart Fridge Assistant helps solve these problems by:

* reducing food waste
* saving time
* saving money
* making shopping easier
* supporting a more eco-friendly lifestyle

## Main Features

### 1. Expiry Date Tracking
The system tracks expiry dates and sends reminders before food goes bad.

### 2. Automatic Shopping List
The fridge creates a shopping list based on items that are missing, low, or expired.

### 3. Recipe Suggestions
The app suggests simple recipes using ingredients that are already in the fridge.

## Website Aim
The aim of the website is to:

* introduce the Smart Fridge Assistant
* explain how it works
* show its main features
* show the benefits for users
* encourage people to buy the product

## Website Structure

### Page 1 – Home
This page will introduce the product.
It will include:

* the product name
* a short slogan
* a short introduction
* an image or illustration of the product
* the main benefits
* a button such as **Learn More** or **Buy Now**

### Page 2 – How It Works

This page will explain how the device works.
It will include:
* the expiry date tracking system
* the automatic shopping list feature
* recipe suggestions
* information on how to buy the product

### Page 3 – Order Now
This page will be the product purchase and checkout page.
It will include:

* a short order summary with product name and price
* product options (colour or bundle selection)
* a delivery details form
* an order confirmation button
* trust signals (warranty, free shipping, returns policy)
* payment information note

## Design Style

The website should have a modern and simple design.
I want it to look:
* clean
* fresh
* easy to use
* organised
* suitable for a smart home product

I plan to use:
* light colours
* simple typography
* clear sections
* images or icons
* easy navigation

## Conclusion

This project will be a 3-page website for a fictional IoT product. The website will clearly explain what Smart Fridge Assistant is, how it works, and why it is useful for students, families, and busy people.
